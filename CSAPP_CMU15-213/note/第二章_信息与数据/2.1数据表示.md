## 整型(integer)
- 无符号数B2U(X) = $\sum^{n-1}_{i=0} x_i 2^i$
- 有符号数B2T(X) = $-x_{n-1}2^{n-1} + \sum^{n-2}_{i=0} x_i 2^i$
